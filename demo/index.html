About

I enjoy bold minimalist design for landing pages, marketing stuff and mobile apps.

I'm focused on user experience as I think it's really essential in changing environment. I know HTML/CSS at basic level, to keep my work consistent.

I have worked for various companies both in the office and from home, on long-term projects and on multiple tasks at a time. OneAccess, Sephora, Skolkovo Technopark are just some of my clients. My clients mostly are from USA and we have no communication issues, my English is fluent. Tasks are really different. I worked a lot with big enterprise applications and know well how to handle them.

I use Adobe tools professionally, Photoshop and Illustrator are my favorite. I also know Dreamweaver, Adobe After Effects and Indesign, prototyping tools (InVision, Sketch), workflows, user stories and mockups (Balsamiq). I can make a complicated application/website design from scratch.

My education is connected with Biology and Psychology. I worked for 2 years as psychologist after my graduation. Deep knowledge in this area really helps me to predict and affect user's behaviour. I know what catches one's attention and keep it, how to make people buy things.

I regular upgrading my UI/UX design skills and knowledges in related design fields - hand lettering, fashion illustration, watercolor painting and stock photography. I'm really happy when project require something above average.
